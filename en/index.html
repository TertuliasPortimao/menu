About Us

Tasty blend of Portuguese and Vietnamese quality food and atmosphere


Tertúlias is a cozy and inviting restaurant located in the picturesque town of Portimão. Our menu features a variety of delicious and authentic dishes from Vietnam and Portuguese, including specialties that are sure to please your palate. Alongside our traditional Vietnamese fare, we also offer a wide selection of bubble tea, fruit tea and coffee, as well as delicious Portuguese dishes. Our specialty dish of local is the famous Francesinha. Come experience our warm and friendly atmosphere enjoy a delicious meal at Tertúlias.
